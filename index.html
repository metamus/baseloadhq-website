// our process
How We Vet Candidates
Every worker we place goes through a structured qualification process.
📄

Credential Verification

We verify trade licenses, OSHA cards, NFPA 70E certifications, and any role-specific credentials before a candidate is presented.

📞

Reference Checks

We contact previous employers or supervisors to confirm work history, reliability, and technical competence.

🔍

Background Screening

Standard background checks are conducted to meet the security and compliance requirements of data center facilities.

🗣

Technical Interview

We assess data center knowledge — redundancy concepts, safety protocols, and hands-on experience relevant to the role.

⚠

Safety Orientation

Every worker receives a safety briefing covering site-specific requirements, PPE standards, and emergency procedures.

🗺

Ongoing Support

We stay in contact throughout the placement. If there's an issue, we address it quickly — we don't just place and disappear.
